About

I am a full stack developer who loves to learn for the sake of learning. While my background in teaching and HR may seem very different from programming on the surface, my passion for all three have been rooted in a desire to help people figure out streamlined solutions to complex problems. As I continue to grow my knowledge, I am looking for software developer opportunities in the Orlando, FL area that emphasize a range of programming tools to create meaningful software for users.

In my free time, I enjoy my fair share of both outdoor and indoor activities. I love to hike, kayak, and take road trips to any place with delicious food - so far, St. Louis has the best barbecue!
