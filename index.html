About

I am a full stack developer who is always learning something new: whether it's a new framework or a new guitar chord. While my background in teaching and HR may seem very different from programming on the surface, my passion for all three have been rooted in a desire to help people figure out streamlined solutions to complex problems. As I continue to grow my knowledge, I am open to software development opportunities in the Orlando, FL area that emphasize a range of programming tools to create meaningful software for users.

In my free time, I enjoy my fair share of both outdoor and indoor activities. I love to hike, kayak, and take road trips to any place with delicious food. I am also a (very) amateur guitar player, photographer and newly converted anime fan.
